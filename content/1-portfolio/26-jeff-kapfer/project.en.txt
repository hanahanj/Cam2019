I combine my graphic design influence with my childhood obsession with birds to create vibrant light-hearted paintings inspired by friends, family and everyday life. I have shown my work throughout St. Louis including Houska Gallery, SOHA Gallery, and The Saint Louis Art Fair in Clayton.

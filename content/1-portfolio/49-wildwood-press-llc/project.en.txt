I founded Wildwood Press in 1996. I specialize in collaborative projects with artists from around the world. Known for its custom handmade paper and large format presses, Wildwood Press is a destination for artists who may choose to meet the challenge of an etching press that is capable of printing five-foot by ten-foot images. Dedicated to experimentation and the unexpected, the prints from Wildwood Press strive to redefine what a print can be.

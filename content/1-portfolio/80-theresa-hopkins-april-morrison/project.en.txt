Married partners Theresa Hopkins and April Morrison share studio space in Tower Grove South. They are professional visual artists specializing in fine art, public installations, and community collaboration. Both received art degrees from Fontbonne University with Theresa's emphasis in Art Education and April's in studio art. Theresa's multi-media work is heavy in drawing, printmaking, and photography. April focuses on acrylic painting and 3D paper assemblage. Theresa's work can be seen at various local art fairs - most recently winning Best In Show at Schlafly Art Outside 2018. Theresa and April co-own/operate Ellipsis Studio.

St. Louis Community College expands minds and changes lives every day by offering high-quality educational experiences leading to degrees, certificates, employment, university transfer, and life-long learning. The Art Annex houses Printmaking, Sculpture and Ceramics studios. Programing includes numerous rotating exhibitions, a visiting artist program, and lectures that are open and available to the greater Saint Louis community and region.

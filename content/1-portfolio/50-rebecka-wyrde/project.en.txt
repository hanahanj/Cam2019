I am a St. Louis native with an arts background. My preferred media is digital photography and acrylic paints. I find inspiration from my travels. In May 2017 I graduated with a Master of Science in Nonprofit Business Management and earned a Bachelors of Fine Arts from the University of MO-St. Louis in 2004. I have exhibited artwork at the St. Louis Artists' Guild, Art Saint Louis, and with Stray Rescue's art auction fundraiser Urban Wanderers.

The Zuka Arts Guild seeks to promote knowledge and participation in the visual arts through exhibitions, teaching programs, workshops, seminars and selected public art projects. The Guild is dedicated to artistic development through inter-organizational skill sharing. Zuka contributes to community, educational and cultural enrichment through the promotion and exhibition of art executed by artists of all proficiency levels.

Food is an everyday object that people often neglect, nevertheless it is a necessity for survival. I am fascinated by the way it grows in my garden, and how its place in popular culture has evolved in society. <br>
I make food to wear on the body, drawing awareness to this extraordinary item through body placement, color and use of industrial materials.
